About ONA @ UNC

The Online News Association at The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill serves as the school's chapter of ONA. We hold campus events and help sessions, invite awesome speakers to come talk to us, and usually take a large group networking trip in the spring. We're open to everyone - don't be shy!

Feel free to send us an email if you have any questions and keep tabs on the calendar below that includes all of our upcoming events.
